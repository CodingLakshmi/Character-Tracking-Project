I would screw up, she’d beat the shit out of me and give me time to cry, and then she’d pop back into my room with a big smile and go, ‘Are you ready for dinner?   7. “My mom never gave me an inch. Anytime I got in trouble it was tough love, lectures, punishment, and hidings. Every time. For every infraction. You get that with a lot of black parents.
